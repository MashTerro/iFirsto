CES 2022: Samsung to host keynote on January 4, will focus on ‘Age of Togetherness’

As the tech world is gearing up for the CES 2022 (Consumer Electronics Show) event, Samsung has announced that it will host its keynote on January 4. The keynote will take place at 6:30 PM in the Venetian’s Palazzo Ballroom in Las Vegas, NV.

Microsoft's $16 billion Nuance bid set for EU antitrust approval
